Lady GaGa
Joanne Stefani Germanotta (born March 20, 1986),
best known by her stage name Lady GaGa,
is an Italian-American singer-songwriter and
dance-pop/electronica musician signed to
Interscope Records.

Lady Gaga at Sommarkrysset, Stockholm, Sweden.
Background information

Years active 2007 - present

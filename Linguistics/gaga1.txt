Lady GaGa
Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta (born March
28, 1986), known by her stage name Lady Gaga,
is an American recording artist. She began
performing in the rock music scene of New York
City's Lower East Side in 2003 and enrolled at
New York University's Tisch School of the
Arts. She soon signed with Streamline Records,
an imprint of Interscope Records. During
her early time at Interscope, she worked as a
songwriter for fellow label artists and
captured the attention of Akon, who recognized
her vocal abilities, and signed her to
his own label, Kon Live Distribution.
